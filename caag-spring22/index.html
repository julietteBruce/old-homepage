Seminar on Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry - Spring 2022

For Spring 2022 the national Fellowship of the Ring Seminar will have talks every other Tuesday from 1:00pm - 2:30pm (PST). The Seminar on Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry will occur on the weeks when there is no Fellowship of the Ring talk beginning on January 27th. The goal will be to have two 50-minute talks covering things related to commutative algebra and algebraic geometry.

Time: Alternating Thursdays, 3:00pm - 5:00pm (PST)
Location: TBD
Organizers: Juliette Bruce and David Eisenbud

This event is being organized by Juliette Bruce and David Eisenbud. If you have any questions about the seminar please email juliette.bruce 'at' berkeley.edu.

Talks marked with a † and in gray are part of the national Fellowship of the Ring Seminar.
